Privacy Policy

1. Introduction

Santa Africa Rehabilitation Center is committed to protecting the privacy of individuals who visit our website and engage with our services. This policy outlines how we collect, use, and safeguard your personal data.

We may collect the following types of personal information:

  • Name and contact details (e.g., email address, phone number)

  • Any information you submit via our contact forms or newsletter signup
  • Website usage data (via cookies and analytics tools)
3. How We Use Your Information

Your information helps us to:

  • Process donations and send confirmations

  • Improve our website and services
4. Information Sharing

We do not sell or rent your personal information. We may share your data with trusted third-party providers who help us operate our website or process donations — all under strict confidentiality agreements.

5. Data Security

We implement appropriate security measures to protect your personal data from unauthorized access, disclosure, alteration, or destruction.

6. Cookies

Our site uses cookies to understand how visitors interact with our content. You can disable cookies in your browser settings.
