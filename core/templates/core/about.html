Santa Africa Rehabilitation Centre

Dedicated to alcohol and substance abuse prevention and intervention in Africa.

Our Story

The Santa Africa rehabilitaion center was founded in 2015 by Mr Edward Kibor, a renowned addiction specialist, and Mr. Peter Karani, a community leader with personal experience of addiction. With a goal to create a holistic, culturally sensitive approach to addressing alcohol and substance abuse in Africa

Santa Africa Foundation, a non-profit organization in Africa, is an alcohol and substance abuse prevention and intervention organization. Our Vision “Souls that will never walk the path of self- destruction" and our Mission is “To empower community members on the right and effective knowledge about alcohol and substance.” Santa Africa Foundation offers help to both the afflicted (those addicted to substances abuse) and the affected family members. Since its inception, Santa Africa Foundation has reached over 100,000 people through community and faith based outreaches, schools and colleges programs, youth forums and the media programs. More than 150 addicts have been helped regain their sobriety though our community-based programs. This has gone a long way in alleviating poverty, illiteracy, diseases and all the other socio-economic and spiritual ills associated with addiction.
